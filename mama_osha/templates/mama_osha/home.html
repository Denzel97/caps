Steven Oduor Dede

Also known as Dj Shiti

Steven Oduor Dede, popularly known as Dj Shiti is the embodiment of ‘grass to grace.' He is among the most sought-after comedians in the country alongside his former colleague, Njugush. Shiti started his career in Nakuru and got his first breakthrough courtesy of the Churchill Show (a Kenyan comedy show). Jalang’o, another popular Kenyan comedian, radio host, and MC, is one of Shiti's most significant sources of inspiration. Shiti came to the city (Nairobi) only to be left hanging by some of the people he had trusted with his accommodation. He had no money or job used to sleep on the floor in Koinange Street during the night and rehearse during the day.
